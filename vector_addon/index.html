Rubberband Fly

Rubberband Fly is an advanced flight bypass working on many servers even today. On servers with less strict anti cheats it was capable of flying with 500blocks/sec for a few seconds.
Bed Bomb / Auto Bed

Vector has a fully customizable bed aura with many settings. The module has different modes to chase targets or damage them in holes. The zero-tick place option gives targets no chance to surround themselves with obsidian.
